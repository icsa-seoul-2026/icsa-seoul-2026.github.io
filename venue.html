Location

Siheung Convention Center, South Korea

Seoul National University Siheung Campus is located in Baegot New Town, Siheung City, Gyeonggi-do.
Address: 173, Seouldaehak-ro, Siheung-si, Gyeonggi-do, Republic of Korea

Transportation (From Incheon Int'l Airport)

By Bus

  • Take Bus 7000 at Incheon International Airport Arrivals T2-B1FL or Incheon International Airport Arrivals (T1) bus stop
  • Get off at Baegot Hanshin The Hyu / Hoban The Prime bus stop
    • (Estimated Time: Approx. 35-55 mins)
  • Transfer to Bus 62
  • Get off at Seoul National Univ. Siheung Campus bus stop (3rd stop, Estimated Time: Approx. 4 mins)
