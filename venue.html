VENUE

Location

Siheung Convention Center, South Korea

Seoul National University Siheung Campus is located in Baegot New Town, Siheung City, Gyeonggi-do.
Address: 173, Seouldaehak-ro, Siheung-si, Gyeonggi-do, Republic of Korea

Transportation

From Incheon Int'l Airport (By Car)

  • Depart from Incheon Int'l Airport Terminal 1
  • Take Incheon Int'l Airport Expressway at Sinbul IC
  • At Airport New Town JC, head right toward 'Songdo, Yeongjong, Incheon Bridge'
  • Pass through Incheon Bridge TG (Toll: ₩5,500) and Gojan TG (Toll: ₩1,200)
  • At Jeongwang IC, head right toward 'Wolgot, Baegot, Siheung Smart Hub'
  • Drive 1.8km, then turn right toward 'Baegot New City'
  • Turn left toward 'Seoul Daehak-ro' to arrive at Siheung Campus
  • Estimated Time: Approx. 30 mins / Total Toll: ₩6,700

By Subway

  • Get off at Oido Station (Seoul Subway Line 4)
  • Walk 205m from Exit 1 and 2
  • Take Bus 62 (in the direction of Baegot)
  • Get off at Halla Vivaldi 2nd stop (Takes about 10-15 minutes)

By Bus

  • Get on Bus No. 3500 and then get off at Seoul National University Siheung Campus bus stop
  • Course: Seoul National University Siheung Campus ↔ Seoul National University (Gwanak) Main Gate ↔ Seoul National University Station
  • Time Required: About 40 minutes
  • Fare: 2,800 KRW
